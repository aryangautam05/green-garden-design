Preparing for Your Consultation

To make the most of your time with our garden design specialists, consider gathering the following information before your appointment:

  • Photos or videos of your current outdoor area.
  • A list of features or plants you like or dislike.
  • Ideas or inspiration (magazines, Pinterest boards, etc.).
  • Your preferred style and colors.
  • Any budget or scheduling constraints.

Bringing this information helps us create a personalized design that aligns with your vision and needs from the start.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the garden design process take?

The timeline depends on the project scope but typically ranges from a few weeks to a couple of months. After consultation, we provide a detailed schedule outlining each phase.

Do you provide maintenance services?

Yes, we offer garden maintenance plans to help keep your space healthy and beautiful year-round. Maintenance is available as an optional service included in your design package or as a standalone offering.

Can I customize my garden design throughout the process?

Absolutely! We encourage collaboration at every stage to ensure the design reflects your preferences and lifestyle.

What areas do you serve?

Our services primarily cover Garden City and surrounding regions. Contact us to confirm if your location is within our service area.

Are estimates or quotes free?

Initial consultations and estimates are free of charge. Detailed pricing is provided after site assessment and project scoping.
